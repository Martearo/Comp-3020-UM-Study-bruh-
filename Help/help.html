Getting Started with StudyDen üß≠

StudyDen is designed to quickly connect you with available study spots and rooms on campus. There are two main views:

  • The Main Map (Homepage): Displays large campus areas or buildings, which we call **Study Spots**.
  • The Rooms Page: Accessed by clicking a spot on the map, this shows the reservable **Study Rooms** inside that building.
  • Current Status: We use your device's current time to determine the open/closed status of locations in real-time.

Map & Navigation Controls üó∫Ô∏è

The map provides an interactive view of campus locations. Use the controls and pins to find your next study spot:

  • Red Pins: Each red pin marks a major campus building or **Study Spot**. Click a pin or hover over it to see an information card.
  • Zoom In/Out ( / ): Use these buttons (top right of the map) to adjust your view.
  • Reset View (): Click this button to instantly return the map to its default zoom level and center position.
  • Search Bar: Use the search bar in the main navbar to quickly filter spots by **Building Name**.

Spot & Room Status üü¢/üî¥ & Ratings ‚≠ê

Understanding the location status and user ratings is key to choosing your spot.

Status Indicators

  • Open (Green): The location is currently within its defined operating hours and accessible.
  • Closed (Red): The location is currently outside its operating hours.

Ratings

Ratings are based on user feedback (out of 5.0) and help you gauge the popularity and quality of the study environment. You can submit your own rating after visiting a spot.

Account, Bookmarks, and Reviews üë§

Your sidebar menu () and profile icon give you access to personalized features:

  • Login / Sign Up: Access your account using your profile icon (top right).
  • Bookmarks: Click the icon on any study room to save it to your Bookmarks list for quick access later.
  • My Reviews: View and manage all the ratings and reviews you have submitted for spots and rooms.
